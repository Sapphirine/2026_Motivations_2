# MotiveOps Public Responsible-AI and Workplace AI Policy Corpus

This source list documents the public materials used to build the MotiveOps policy-grounding RAG corpus. The local RAG corpus was manually curated from these public sources into `policy_chunks.json` with 37 policy chunks. In the project repository, the same file also lives at `rag_corpus/policy_chunks.json`.

MotiveOps used this corpus to retrieve policy constraints before generating AI workflow adoption interventions, and to evaluate policy retrieval Recall@5 and constraint uptake across the canonical adoption benchmark.

Included derived corpus file:

- `policy_chunks.json`: 37 manually curated RAG chunks used by MotiveOps for local Chroma retrieval.

## Dataset Description for Submission Forms

The dataset is a public responsible-AI and workplace AI policy corpus used to ground and evaluate MotiveOps recommendations. It was collected manually from official public websites and curated into a 37-chunk local RAG corpus. The sources cover responsible-AI governance, generative-AI risk, privacy and cybersecurity, education and student privacy, workplace AI, HR and employment selection, legal AI use, finance/model-risk management, consumer protection, platform usage policies, and vendor AI governance.

The tested MotiveOps scenarios covered AI tutoring in schools, coding assistants, customer-support copilots, analytics copilots, manager stigma around AI use, legal review AI, sales AI coaching, marketing content AI, finance forecasting AI, and HR policy assistants. The software can also support company-specific AI-use policies, internal security rules, rollout plans, employee survey snippets, training documents, and responsible-AI governance documents.

Term of use: public source materials. Detailed reuse restrictions may vary by source.
